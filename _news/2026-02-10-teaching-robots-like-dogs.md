"Teaching Robots Like Dogs," a collaborative study with [Taerim Yoon](https://taerimyoon.me/) from Korea University, has received media attention from [Tech Xplore (English)](https://techxplore.com/news/2026-01-legged-robots-dogs.html) and [Yonhap News (Korean)](https://www.yna.co.kr/view/AKR20260206115900017). The paper explores agile navigation through luring, gesture, and speech. Read the full paper and see the demonstrations on the [project webpage](https://taerimyoon.me/Teaching-Robots-Like-Dogs-Learning-Agile-Navigation-from-Luring-Gesture-and-Speech/).
